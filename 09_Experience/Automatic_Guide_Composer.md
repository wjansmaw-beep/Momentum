# Automatic Guide Composer

Status: Directional, first deterministic implementation approved  
Version: 1.0

## Purpose

Momentum cannot depend on every future Experience being written by hand. A live nature opportunity, public place, temporary event, route, recipe variation, or generated workout needs the same depth and restraint as a curated Capsule.

The Guide Composer turns an already approved Experience candidate into at most three stage-specific guide moments. It enriches the Capsule after feasibility and selection; it never decides that an unsafe or unsupported Experience should exist.

## Current deterministic pipeline

> Experience candidate -> current evidence -> route contract -> experience-kind contract -> guide moments -> visible composition label

The first implementation is deterministic:

- current evidence may produce one carefully worded live guide moment;
- a route plan may produce one buffer, handoff, or access guide moment;
- the experience kind may provide one reviewed evergreen guide moment;
- existing curated insights are preserved;
- the total remains at most three;
- expired evidence is rejected before composition.

This already lets newly composed Living World cards receive guidance without a hand-authored card for that exact place or observation.

## Future generative provider

A language model may later propose wording or synthesize several approved sources, but only behind the same contract. Its output is a draft until deterministic validation confirms:

- every factual sentence is supported by supplied, current evidence or reviewed editorial knowledge;
- source identifiers and freshness remain attached;
- uncertain observations are phrased as possibilities, never guarantees;
- no private profile reason is exposed inside the shared or public guide content;
- no diagnosis, inaccessible route claim, invented local history, wildlife certainty, or unsafe instruction is introduced;
- the moment fits a real Capsule step and does not exceed the guide-moment limit;
- silence remains valid when grounding is insufficient.

Generated fluency is not evidence. The provider may improve expression, not manufacture reality.

## User-visible truth

Automatically composed guidance is labelled. Current live evidence remains separately inspectable with source and observation time. Editorial and evergreen fallback are named when live coverage is absent.

The person can choose quiet guidance, mute an insight topic, reject the content in reflection, or ignore the guide entirely.

## Global behavior

The Composer must produce a complete Capsule anywhere the underlying Experience is feasible:

- local live coverage adds timely depth;
- reviewed content packs add local character;
- routes add practical composition;
- global evergreen contracts keep the experience useful without pretending local knowledge.

Equal usefulness does not require equal data coverage. Honest fallback is part of quality.
