# ADR-002 — Interaction Slice Approved

Status: Accepted  
Date: 2026-07-10

## Decision

The Founder tested the first application slice and confirmed that its flow works.

The approved interaction sequence is:

> Human room → available time → desired feeling → Experience Promise → preparation → Presence → optional reflection

This validates the interaction model, not the final visual design or content depth.

## What was learned

- Asking for time and a desired feeling is understandable.
- The flow can turn an active human moment into one concrete Experience Promise.
- Acceptance leads directly toward preparation and action rather than more browsing.
- Presence can become markedly quieter than the promise and selection states.

## Consequences

- The sequence remains the baseline for the next prototype iteration.
- Visual refinement may improve atmosphere, continuity, hierarchy, and desire without changing the approved flow.
- Richer content and context-aware ranking remain later work.
- No conclusion is yet drawn about passive moment detection, live Apple context, or the quality of recommendations across different experience types.

## Founder observation

> The flow works. It is naturally not richly filled yet, but the flow is good.
