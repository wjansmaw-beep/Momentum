# ADR-035 — Adaptive active intent and Meaning Thread continuity

Status: Accepted and first local implementation prototyped  
Date: 2026-07-15

## Decision

Discover interprets free expression before selecting an Experience. It asks at most one clarification and only when that answer materially changes feasibility, company, equipment, experience form, or direction. The question uses human outcomes and executable choices rather than exposing a category catalogue. The explicit answer is scoped to the current Human Moment and outranks durable preference inference.

A user-confirmed near intention, growth direction, or meaning anchor may form a Meaning Thread through an eligible Experience. A thread appears only when the person's own words have a direct or reviewed conceptual relationship with the Experience. It can be visible in the Promise, Preparation, and Memory, but never becomes a quota, score, obligation, identity judgment, or fabricated purpose claim.

The first new complete blueprint enabled by this decision is a material-free bodyweight session. It contains preparation, safe adaptation, warm-up, staged exercises, finish, optional learning, and Memory language. It passes the movement domain contract before ranking.

## Interaction contract

1. The person states available time and optional free intent.
2. Momentum interprets the words locally.
3. If one answer materially changes the result, Momentum asks one question.
4. The answer is added only to this moment's intent.
5. Blueprint validation, guide composition, quality audit, and ranking still run in order.
6. One primary Experience and one meaningfully different alternative remain the maximum result surface.

## Meaning Thread contract

- The source is always a direction the person explicitly entered and has not paused.
- No thread is shown when the relationship is too weak.
- The interface says that the relationship is soft and user-confirmed.
- Memory may ask whether the Experience touched that direction, but does not assume that it did.
- Deleting or pausing the direction removes it from future Experience composition.

## Trust and privacy

- Interpretation and clarification remain device-local in this prototype.
- Unknown wording produces clarification rather than invented certainty.
- Explicit company language may update only the current Discover context.
- A clarification does not silently alter the durable personal profile.
- No external model, backend, health inference, or private communication source is introduced.

## Boundaries

- Natural-language understanding remains deterministic and Dutch-first.
- Concept mappings are reviewed product rules, not claims about hidden psychological needs.
- Broader language and cultural interpretation require localization and evaluation.
- Generative drafts remain behind ADR-034 domain validators and the candidate quality gate.

## Evidence sought

Determine whether people understand the single clarification, receive a visibly better Experience, recognize a Meaning Thread without feeling judged, and can still begin quickly.
