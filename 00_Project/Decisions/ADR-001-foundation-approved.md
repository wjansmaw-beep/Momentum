# ADR-001 — Foundation Approved for First Prototype

Status: Accepted  
Date: 2026-07-10

## Decision

The Founder approved moving from document foundation to the first bounded application prototype.

The approved implementation scope is:

> Now → I have time → time choice → one desired-outcome question → one Experience Promise → correction → preparation → Presence

The first implemented outcome is the challenge scenario from `08_Design/North_Star_Active_Flow.md`.

## Constraints

- Use local, visibly scenario-based data.
- Do not claim access to Apple, health, calendar, weather, location, or Living World context.
- Do not add backend, accounts, payments, passive detection, or generative AI.
- Do not recreate old PRIMX modules or navigation.
- Keep the implementation small enough to replace after product learning.

## Consequences

Application code may now be added. The Constitution remains authoritative, and the prototype is evidence for design review rather than a declaration that the UI is final.
