# ADR-034 — Experience Blueprint Composer and consumer surface cleanup

Status: Accepted and first deterministic implementation prototyped  
Date: 2026-07-15

## Decision

Momentum separates complete Experience composition from Guide composition. Explicit intent may select a reviewed domain blueprint for outside, food, movement, recovery, connection, situated learning, or culture. The blueprint produces or adapts a complete Experience before the existing Guide Composer enriches it, the candidate quality gate audits it, and ranking selects it.

Every domain owns a validator. Route and outside experiences protect time, source freshness and access boundaries. Food protects ingredient and allergy boundaries. Movement protects equipment and safe adaptation. Learning requires a recognizable source type. Other domains retain complete execution and non-manipulative promises. A future generative provider may only submit drafts behind the same validators; no external model is activated by this decision.

The consumer flow is made quieter at the same time:

- Now uses kind-specific feasibility labels instead of calling every experience a walk;
- Prepare applies sensible defaults and keeps company, sharing and guide-depth changes behind an explicit adjustment;
- Remember asks for one lightweight outcome and optional sentence before exposing detailed learning controls;
- muted guidance does not reappear in the Meaning Thread;
- Profile contains personal direction, memory and connections, while prototype diagnostics live in a collapsed Momentum Lab.

## Why

Automatic guide moments make existing Experiences richer but do not create the complete route, recipe, workout, family moment or cultural experience the person asked for. Blueprint composition adds breadth without allowing ungrounded free generation. Quieter preparation and reflection protect the transition into and out of real life.

## Autonomy and trust

- The person's current words outrank inferred preferences.
- Composition remains visible in plain language without exposing internal scoring as primary UI.
- Generated drafts are never eligible merely because a model returned them.
- Detailed reflection remains optional and reversible.
- Diagnostics never masquerade as a consumer benefit.

## Boundaries

- The first composer is deterministic and reuses reviewed complete Experience material.
- It does not call a model, create a backend, infer private context, or claim universal live-source coverage.
- Domain validators are eligibility boundaries, not medical, legal, nutritional or navigational guarantees.
- Worldwide operation still requires localization, regional coverage reporting and reviewed source adapters.

## Next evidence

Test whether people can express varied intent in their own words, understand the composed result, begin with fewer preparation choices, and finish Memory without feeling surveyed.
