# ADR-003 — Desired Feelings Produce Distinct Capsules

Status: Accepted for prototype validation  
Date: 2026-07-10

## Decision

The same open Human Moment may lead to materially different experiences when the user chooses a different desired feeling.

The prototype therefore supports distinct paths for:

- calm → a guided reset;
- energy → a brief movement reset;
- wonder → a quiet observation prompt;
- connection → one conversation invitation;
- challenge → a kettlebell session;
- choose for me → a permission-light unfamiliar route.

These are separate Experience Capsules, not one activity with different emotional labels.

## Time behavior

The chosen time window determines the displayed experience commitment and protected return buffer. The prototype uses a simple local calculation; it does not yet claim intelligent duration prediction.

## Presence behavior

Guided experiences retain only essential timing and cues. Experiences that should belong to the person or people involved enter a quiet state and wait for the user to return.

## Boundaries

- All content remains local and scenario-based.
- No Apple, calendar, health, location, weather, or Living World context is claimed.
- The experience set is evidence for interaction design, not a finished recommendation library.
- Ranking quality and passive moment detection remain unvalidated.
