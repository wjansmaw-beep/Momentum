# ADR-054 — Premium coherence and consumer language

Status: Accepted and implemented  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2026-07-18

## Problem

The warm daylight direction improved the visual character, but consumer surfaces still exposed evaluation language, equal-weight card stacks and technical provenance. This made the prototype visible before the experience. A few content mismatches also weakened trust: a personal Meaning Thread could be shown without a credible relationship to the selected experience, and timer duration could contradict the step label.

## Decision

Momentum applies one premium-coherence rule across Now, Today, Discover, Life Book and the Capsule flow:

1. Consumer surfaces describe human value and current context; generator, fixture, proof and evaluation language belongs in the Founder Lab.
2. Now keeps one primary Experience Promise and makes direct alternatives finite and visually quiet.
3. Today is an editorial day rhythm: the nearest opportunity receives primary weight and later opportunities become progressively quieter.
4. Discover begins with time, free language and a few contextual examples instead of presenting a category form.
5. Life Book leads with memories; counts and learning remain secondary and user-correctable.
6. A Meaning Thread is shown only when its wording has a credible direct or conceptual relationship with the selected experience.
7. Promise and Prepare use plain consumer language. Internal terms such as blueprint, composer, coverage and total commitment are not primary labels.
8. Timed Presence steps match their declared duration. Advancing early is an explicit skip, not an ambiguous primary action.
9. The consultable guide is a temporary sheet over Presence, so the current experience remains perceptually continuous.
10. Main-surface navigation uses one code-native icon grammar, readable labels and explicit scroll restoration.
11. Photography receives an adaptive two-layer contrast treatment while the light application frame stays visually quiet.

## Consequences

Founder evaluation remains available in Profile but no longer competes with the Experience Promise. The product should feel less like an AI prototype and more like a calm editorial companion. Personal relevance earns trust by abstaining from weak explanations. Today and Life Book remain rich without becoming engagement feeds.

Physical-device validation is still required for Dynamic Type, reduced motion, image loading, gesture behavior and native material rendering.
