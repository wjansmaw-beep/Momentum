# ADR-012: Discovery is intentional and separate from Now

Status: Accepted  
Date: 2026-07-11

## Context

A richer visual direction showed alternative cards beneath the primary suggestion. While attractive, that pattern risks turning `Now` into a feed and competing with the single Experience Promise.

## Decision

`Now` contains one primary suggestion and no row of alternative experiences.

Broader discovery may later become a consciously opened, finite and contextual place. It does not appear as a feed beneath the primary promise. The immediate correction action “Iets anders past beter” remains part of the suggestion flow and is not a substitute for open-ended browsing.

The initial primary navigation remains focused on `Nu`, `Leven` and `Profiel`. A dedicated Discovery destination is deferred until its bounded purpose and screen-time behavior are designed and tested.

## Consequences

- Alternative cards, social proof and “Bekijk alles” do not belong on the `Now` surface.
- Discovery must be inspiration-led, finite and explicitly entered.
- Deferring its navigation keeps the first product focused on contextual action.
