# ADR-048 — Generation Variation and Separate Evaluation

Status: Accepted and implemented  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2026-07-18

## Problem

Visible Generator Evaluation Mode proves the complete technical route, but equal context can produce the same demonstration repeatedly. General prototype counters also cannot answer whether a newly synthesized Capsule felt personal, surprising, executable, or useful. Reusing personal-profile learning for this purpose would mix product-quality research with durable user preference.

## Decision

The generator contract accepts a bounded, non-identifying `variationSeed`. It may change the wording and complete execution of a Capsule, but it cannot widen the domain, add permissions, supply live facts, or bypass validation.

The controlled fixture contains two complete variants for each approved experience kind: outside, food, movement, restore, connect, learn, and culture. Time, day part, company, and explicitly available equipment remain practical constraints rather than material for personal inference. Every result still requires preparation, multiple Presence steps, a truthful promise, a memory prompt, and relevant domain guardrails.

After a generated Capsule is completed, Memory may collect four optional local evaluation signals:

- felt personal;
- was surprising;
- was directly executable;
- the content helped.

These signals are stored as aggregate prototype evidence and shown in Momentum Lab. They do not alter the personal profile, ranking affinity, reflections, memories, directions, or muted topics.

## Grounding boundary

Generation still receives no location or live source payload. Verified Living World evidence and place knowledge may be attached only after generation by their owning adapters, with original provenance and expiry. A variation seed is never evidence.

## Autonomy and interface

`Now` continues to show one primary promise at a time. A new explicit Momentmaker action creates one candidate; it does not create an endless feed. The ordinary finite next-card interaction remains the autonomy mechanism for alternative proposals.

## Evaluation interpretation

Momentum Lab distinguishes drafts accepted by the capsule contract and shown, drafts rejected by validation, and completed generated Capsules that received optional quality signals. These local counts support Founder evaluation only. They are not production analytics or representative user research.
