# ADR-055 — Living Interface and contextual opening

Status: Accepted for prototype implementation  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2026-07-18

## Problem

The current consumer flow contains the right product concepts, but presents them as a sequence of separate pages and repeated generic cards. Promise and Prepare repeat the same proposition, Today gives every moment a similar photographic weight, and Discover and Life Book feel less complete than Now. The result does not yet feel like one premium companion that changes shape around a living experience.

The opening line is also static. The Founder wants it eventually to become a personal day line: a calm, truthful sentence that reflects the time of day and what matters now, possibly with the emotional role of an affirmation. Its source, safety and degree of inference are not yet decided.

## Decision

Momentum adopts one **Living Interface** across the complete journey:

1. A suggestion opens directly into one adaptive invitation-and-preparation surface. Promise and Prepare no longer exist as two repeated consumer pages.
2. The selected experience keeps its imagery, language and identity while the interface transforms from possibility to readiness, Presence and Memory.
3. Capsule forms share visual principles but not a generic layout. Routes emphasize destination and arrival, workouts emphasize sequence and effort, recipes emphasize ingredients and preparation, and recovery emphasizes rhythm and quiet.
4. Now remains one visible primary suggestion with a finite direct alternative control. The active-intent entrance stays easy to find without competing with the suggestion.
5. Today uses one current or next moment as its editorial lead. Other moments become compact chapters rather than repeated hero cards.
6. Discover begins as a contextual invitation to express available time and direction, not as an empty form or content library.
7. Life Book is memory-led and visually complete without becoming a performance dashboard or social feed.
8. Presence remains both consultable and easy to put away. Controls must be explicit, accessible and visually calm.
9. The static opening copy remains unchanged in this implementation. A contextual day line is recorded as an open product question and must not silently infer mood, health, purpose or personal truth.

## Contextual day line boundary

A future day line may combine permitted context such as day part, weather, known free space, a user-confirmed direction and a recent explicit memory. It must:

- distinguish observed context from interpretation;
- avoid diagnosis, fate, obligation and false intimacy;
- remain useful with no optional permissions;
- offer variety without pretending certainty;
- be easy to mute or simplify;
- never use affirmation language to manipulate action.

Until that language system is approved, the current neutral opening remains the truthful fallback.

## Consequences

The product should feel richer because each stage becomes more specific, not because more modules or feeds are added. Navigation and typography remain one system, photography carries emotional color, and supporting information is progressively disclosed. Existing decision, evidence, sharing, guide and reflection contracts remain unchanged.
