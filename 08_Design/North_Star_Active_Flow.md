# North Star Active Flow

Status: Directional visual specification  
Version: 1.0

## Scenario

> The user unexpectedly has one hour at work and wants a challenge.

This flow visualizes the complete transition from active intent to Presence. It is a North Star concept, not a final component or pixel specification.

## Frame 1 — Now

The current Experience Promise occupies the visual center. A quiet secondary action reads:

> Ik heb tijd nu

It preserves agency without turning the surface into navigation or a catalogue.

## Frame 2 — Time

A compact sheet rises from the same visual world:

> Hoeveel tijd kwam er vrij?

Choices: 15 min, 30 min, 1 uur, 2 uur, tot…

The selected hour remains visible through the rest of the flow and is never requested again.

## Frame 3 — Desired outcome

Because the prototype has limited context, it asks one clarifying question:

> Wat zou dit uur de moeite waard maken?

The user selects **Mezelf uitdagen**. Other feelings remain calm, energy, surprise, and connection. These are outcomes, not activity categories.

## Frame 4 — Experience Promise

The sheet dissolves back into a single cinematic promise:

> Een sterk halfuur, zonder gehaast terug te komen.

Distinctive detail:

> Eén kettlebell is genoeg voor een complete sessie hier.

Compact feasibility:

- 30 min training;
- 10 min preparation and finish;
- 20 min protected buffer.

Primary action: **Zet deze training klaar**  
Correction: **Iets anders past beter**

“Why this fits” remains secondary: one hour, at work, challenge explicitly chosen, available equipment represented as scenario context.

## Frame 5 — Preparation

The promise expands rather than navigating to an unrelated page. Persuasion stops.

Show only:

- one kettlebell;
- water;
- enough floor space;
- start when ready.

Primary action: **Ik ben klaar**

## Frame 6 — Presence

The interface becomes almost empty:

- current movement;
- current interval or repetitions;
- total progress;
- pause/stop;
- subtle haptic timing.

No other recommendations, navigation, streaks, metrics, or content appear.

The final state quietly says:

> Klaar. Neem de rest van je uur terug.

An optional later reflection asks:

> Was dit een goede besteding van je uur?

## Visual direction

- premium editorial realism rather than fitness-dashboard aesthetics;
- dark mineral blue, warm amber, soft bone, and restrained natural green;
- one strong image or atmospheric canvas per promise, never decorative clutter;
- generous negative space and large humane typography;
- translucent materials used sparingly for depth, not trend imitation;
- continuity through shared light, shape, and movement;
- no visible “AI” branding or chat bubbles;
- reduced-motion equivalent retains the full hierarchy.

## Evaluation

The flow succeeds when the user can feel:

1. I have room.
2. I remain in control.
3. This experience fits the real hour.
4. I want to begin.
5. Momentum now becomes quiet.
