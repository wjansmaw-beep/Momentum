# Wonder

Status: Foundational  
Version: 1.0

Wonder answers:

> What meaningful or beautiful thing could happen if I choose this?

It is the bridge between a relevant recommendation and genuine desire to act.

## Hidden Gems are emotional promises

A Hidden Gem is not merely an unknown place. It is the distinctive, credible detail that makes an experience feel worth living now.

Examples include:

- a viewpoint where the evening light arrives at the right time;
- a recently observed bird or a seasonal bloom;
- a small cultural event nearby;
- the ingredient that turns a familiar meal into something memorable;
- the calm likely to follow a breathing exercise;
- the satisfying final movement of a workout;
- a simple prompt that deepens time with another person.

## Experience Promise

Every selected experience should have one primary promise:

- **Discovery:** wonder, surprise, freedom;
- **Recovery:** calm, relief, restoration;
- **Movement:** strength, vitality, pride;
- **Nourishment:** energy, comfort, enjoyment;
- **Connection:** closeness, attention, belonging;
- **Growth:** curiosity, competence, insight.

These are lenses, not old product modules or navigation categories.

## Promise composition

A strong promise combines:

1. **felt outcome** — how the person may feel;
2. **specific detail** — what makes this experience distinctive;
3. **immediacy** — why this possibility exists now;
4. **truthfulness** — confidence and source quality match the wording.

Example:

> “The wind is expected to drop near sunset, and the sea aster is in bloom. In 45 minutes you could be at a quiet viewpoint over the water.”

## Ethical wonder

Momentum must never manufacture false urgency, invent sightings, overstate health effects, or disguise advertising as personal relevance.

Use calibrated language:

- confirmed: “reported today” with source and time;
- probable: “good chance” with evidence;
- inspirational: “you may notice” without presenting it as fact.

When reliable live detail is unavailable, a timeless but honest promise is better than fabricated novelty.

## Presentation order

The primary surface should generally show:

1. the future experience or felt outcome;
2. the most compelling truthful detail;
3. the action;
4. compact practical facts;
5. an optional explanation of why it fits.

Wonder invites action. It must not become more content to consume.
